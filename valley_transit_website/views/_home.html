Who are we?

Valley Transit is a public transportation business that operates in Liberty County, Georgia. We provide a frequent bus service to the places you want. We also provide taxis and limousines for direct transportation. Valley Transit was founded by Destiny White on the 4th of October 2022 and has been providing transportation services since.
